Whether you are moving a family house or you need a studio removal, we can tailor a moving package to suit your requirements. We do full packing and unpacking service and take care of customs formalities. If you’re interested in packing your household goods yourself, we will provide you with all the packing materials you may need.

Depending on your budget you can choose either individual door to door trucks or containers, or use our cost-efficient combi freight options. In case you urgently need some of your belongings, we can have them sent by air freight instead.
